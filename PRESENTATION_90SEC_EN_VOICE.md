# Memento Mori - Text for ChatGPT Voice (90 seconds)

## Format with pauses and emotional cues

**Instruction:** Copy the text below into ChatGPT and ask it to create a voice version with the specified pauses and emotions.

---

## FULL TEXT WITH CUES

[EMOTION: quietly, slowly, intimately, with pain]

When a loved one passes away...

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

You're left alone with grief.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

And in this very moment, when you're most vulnerable...

[PAUSE: 1 second]

You're forced to make decisions.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

Expensive decisions. Complex decisions.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

In scattered places. With non-transparent prices. Without support.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: louder, with force, decisively]

This is wrong.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: softer, thoughtfully, intriguingly]

We thought...

[PAUSE: 1 second]

What if everything could be different?

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: loudly, with weight, as a brand, dramatically]

Memento Mori.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: confidently, energetically, building up]

A platform that unites everything.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

Choice of faith and rituals. All goods and services. Will. Funeral organization.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

In one place.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

With transparent prices.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

With document automation.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

With the ability to plan in advance.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

Freeing loved ones from difficult decisions.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: slowly, mysteriously, intriguingly]

This is not just a platform.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: loudly, dramatically, climax, maximum energy]

This is a transformation.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: slowly, building, contrast]

What was once chaos...

[PAUSE: 1 second]

[EMOTION: lighter, more positive]

Becomes order.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

What was once pain...

[PAUSE: 1 second]

Becomes support.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

What was once non-transparent...

[PAUSE: 1 second]

Becomes clarity.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: slowly, philosophically, with weight, return to intimacy]

Memento Mori.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

And be prepared for it.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

Transformation of a painful process into a transparent, organized, and protected ecosystem.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

Where a person plans important matters in advance.

[PAUSE: 1 second]

And loved ones receive support and clarity.

[PAUSE: 2 seconds]

[EMOTION: quietly, softly, conclusively]

Thank you.
