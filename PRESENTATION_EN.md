## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Timing

- Total duration: ~4 minutes
- More time on emotional moments
- Pauses are critical - let the audience feel

### Visual Style

- **Colors:** Black, white, gold (#D4AF37), red (for problems)
- **Typography:** Elegant, readable
- **Animations:** Smooth, emotional
- **Transitions:** Soft, respectful

### Delivery Tips

- **Pace:** Slow, respectful
- **Pauses:** Long, especially on emotional moments
- **Voice:** Warm, empathetic, but confident
- **Eye contact:** Important - look into the audience's eyes
- **Emotions:** Show that you understand the pain

### Key Moments for Emphasis

- "In the moment when you are most vulnerable" (repeated)
- "This is wrong"
- "Memento Mori. And be prepared for it."
- "Transformation of a painful process"
