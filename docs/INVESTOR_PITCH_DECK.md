## Executive Summary

**Memento Mori** is a comprehensive marketplace platform connecting clients with verified funeral service providers, while also offering free will preparation services. We solve the critical problem of organizing funeral services during times of grief by providing transparency, security, and convenience in one unified platform.

### Key Highlights

- **Market Size:** $50-70B annual market (Europe & USA)
- **Unique Value:** Only platform combining funeral services + will preparation
- **Revenue Model:** 10-20% commission on transactions
- **Target:** $20-30M revenue by Year 3
- **Ask:** $1-2M Seed Round

---

## Problem Statement

### The Pain Points

1. **Stress and Confusion During Grief**
   - People don't know where to turn when organizing funerals
   - No centralized platform for funeral services
   - Difficulty verifying provider reliability
   - Lack of price transparency

2. **Industry Opacity**
   - Hard to compare prices and services
   - No reliable review system
   - Risk of fraud and poor service quality
   - No protection mechanisms for consumers

3. **Complex Dual Challenge**
   - Organizing funerals + preparing wills simultaneously
   - Multiple disconnected services
   - No unified solution for all needs

### Market Impact

- **10-12 million deaths annually** in Europe and USA
- **Average funeral cost:** $5,000-7,000
- **Total market value:** $50-70 billion per year
- **Digital adoption:** Growing, but no dominant platform exists

## Solution

### Memento Mori Platform

A **comprehensive marketplace** that combines:

1. **Funeral Services Marketplace**
   - Verified vendor directory (services, products, flowers, monuments)

2. **Will Preparation Service**
   - **Free will preparation** when funeral rights are transferred

## Business Model

### Primary Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction Commission (10-20%)**
   - Commission on each service/product order
   - Average transaction: $5,500-7,700
   - Commission per transaction: $550-1,540
   - **Projected:** 80% of total revenue

2. **Premium Services**
   - Funeral insurance products
   - Premium customer support
   - Priority listing for vendors
   - Additional legal services
   - **Projected:** 15% of total revenue

3. **Subscription Fees (Future)**
   - Vendor subscription plans
   - Premium vendor features
   - **Projected:** 5% of total revenue
