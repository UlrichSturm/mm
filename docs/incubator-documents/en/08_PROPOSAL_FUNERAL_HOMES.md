## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much does it cost to join?**
A: There are no upfront costs. We operate on a commission-based model - you only pay when you receive bookings.

**Q: How are clients matched to partners?**
A: Clients find partners through geographic search, service categories, and price comparison on the platform.

**Q: What happens if a client cancels?**
A: Our cancellation policy protects both parties. Cancellation terms are clearly defined in the service agreement.

**Q: How do payments work?**
A: Payments are processed through our secure escrow system. Funds are held until service completion, then released to you.

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**
A: Yes, you have full control over your service pricing. The platform helps you present your prices transparently.

**Q: How do reviews work?**
A: Clients can leave reviews after service completion. You can respond to reviews and build your reputation on the platform.
