# Social Media Marketing Plan - Memento Mori

**Date:** 2025-12-07
**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Social Media Strategy
**Language:** English

---

## Overview

This document outlines the social media marketing strategy for Memento Mori, focusing on LinkedIn and Facebook as primary platforms for B2B and B2C engagement.

---

## Executive Summary

**Objective:** Build brand awareness, establish thought leadership, and drive user acquisition through strategic social media presence on LinkedIn and Facebook.

**Target Platforms:**

- LinkedIn (Primary - B2B focus)
- Facebook (Primary - B2C focus)

**Timeline:** 12-month plan with quarterly reviews

**Budget:** €15,000-25,000 annually (Year 1)

---

## Platform Strategy

### LinkedIn

**Purpose:** B2B engagement, vendor acquisition, thought leadership, investor relations

**Target Audience:**

- Funeral service providers
- Lawyers and notaries
- Business owners in funeral industry
- Investors and incubators
- Industry professionals

**Content Focus:**

- Industry insights and trends
- Business development content
- Vendor success stories
- Platform updates and features
- Thought leadership articles
- Company news and milestones

**Posting Frequency:**

- 3-5 posts per week
- 1-2 long-form articles per month
- Daily engagement with comments and messages

**Best Times to Post:**

- Tuesday-Thursday: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM
- Monday: 8-10 AM
- Friday: 8-9 AM

### Facebook

**Purpose:** B2C engagement, client acquisition, community building, brand awareness

**Target Audience:**

- Families planning funerals
- Individuals planning wills
- General public (40-70 years old)
- Caregivers and family members

**Content Focus:**

- Educational content about funeral planning
- Will preparation information
- Customer testimonials
- Community support content
- Platform features and benefits
- Helpful tips and guides

**Posting Frequency:**

- 5-7 posts per week
- 1-2 video posts per week
- Daily community engagement

**Best Times to Post:**

- Monday-Friday: 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM
- Saturday-Sunday: 10 AM-12 PM

## Content Creation Process

### Workflow

1. **Planning (Monthly)**
   - Review monthly theme
   - Plan content calendar
   - Identify key dates and events
   - Assign content creators

2. **Creation (Weekly)**
   - Write content (Monday-Tuesday)
   - Create visuals (Tuesday-Wednesday)
   - Review and approve (Wednesday)
   - Schedule posts (Thursday)

3. **Publishing (Daily)**
   - Publish scheduled posts
   - Monitor engagement
   - Respond to comments
   - Track performance

4. **Analysis (Weekly/Monthly)**
   - Review metrics
   - Identify top-performing content
   - Adjust strategy
   - Report to team

## Crisis Management

### Response Protocol

**Negative Comments:**

1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Respond professionally
3. Take conversation offline if needed
4. Document and report

**Crisis Situations:**

1. Assess severity
2. Notify management
3. Prepare response
4. Communicate transparently
5. Monitor and follow up

### Guidelines

- Always respond professionally
- Never delete negative comments (unless spam/hate)
- Be transparent and honest
- Apologize when appropriate
- Take responsibility
