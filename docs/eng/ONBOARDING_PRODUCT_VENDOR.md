## Overview

This guide helps product vendors (selling physical goods like caskets, urns, flowers, monuments) join the Memento Mori platform.

### What You Can Sell

- Caskets and coffins
- Urns and cremation products
- Funeral flowers
- Monuments and headstones
- Memorial products
- Other funeral-related products

---

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Apply

1. Contact platform admin
2. Submit business information
3. Wait for approval

### Step 2: Account Creation

1. Admin creates your vendor account
2. You receive email with login credentials
3. Complete profile setup

---

## Profile Setup

### Required Information

- **Business Information:**
  - Business name
  - Business registration number
  - Tax ID
  - Business address
  - Contact information

- **Product Information:**
  - Product categories
  - Product range
  - Pricing structure
  - Delivery options

- **Operational Information:**
  - Inventory management
  - Shipping policies
  - Return policies
  - Customer service

## Product Listing

### Creating Product Listings

1. **Go to Services Page:**
   - Navigate to Vendor Portal
   - Click "Create Service"

2. **Fill Product Details:**
   - Product name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - Images
   - Specifications

3. **Set Availability:**
   - Stock quantity
   - Delivery time
   - Shipping options

4. **Submit for Review:**
   - Admin reviews listing
   - Approval/rejection

### Product Requirements

- **High-Quality Images:** Clear product photos
- **Accurate Descriptions:** Detailed product information
- **Competitive Pricing:** Market-appropriate prices
- **Complete Information:** All required fields

## Order Management

### Processing Orders

1. **View Orders:**
   - Go to Orders page
   - See all product orders
   - Filter by status

2. **Update Order Status:**
   - Confirm order
   - Mark as processing
   - Mark as shipped
   - Mark as delivered

3. **Handle Issues:**
   - Process cancellations
   - Handle returns
   - Resolve disputes

### Order Fulfillment

- **Quick Processing:** Process orders within 24 hours
- **Accurate Shipping:** Provide tracking information
- **Quality Control:** Ensure product quality
- **Customer Communication:** Keep customers informed

## Getting Paid

### Payment Process

1. **Order Payment:**
   - Client pays through platform
   - Funds held in escrow

2. **Order Completion:**
   - Mark order as delivered
   - Platform releases payment

3. **Payment Schedule:**
   - Weekly payouts
   - Automatic transfers
   - Payment history available

### Commission Structure

- Platform commission: 10-20% per transaction
- Payment processing fees: 2.9% + $0.25
- Net payout: Order total minus fees
