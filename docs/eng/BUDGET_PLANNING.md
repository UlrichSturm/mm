### Break-even Analysis

**Break-even Point:** Month 10-12

**Break-even Revenue:** ~$1.5M annually

**Break-even Transactions:** ~2,500-3,000 per year

**Calculation:**
- Fixed Costs: $686,400/year
- Variable Costs: ~15% of revenue
- Required Revenue: $686,400 / (1 - 0.15) = $807,529
- With margin: ~$1.5M to cover all costs + growth

## Risk Analysis

### Financial Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|:------:|:-----------:|------------|
| **Revenue Below Projections** | High | Medium | Conservative estimates, multiple revenue streams |
| **Cost Overruns** | High | Medium | Regular monitoring, contingency fund |
| **Payment Processing Issues** | Medium | Low | Multiple payment providers, fallback plans |
| **Market Competition** | Medium | Medium | Unique features, fast execution |
| **Regulatory Changes** | High | Low | Legal consultation, compliance focus |

### Contingency Planning

**Reserve Fund:** $50K - $400K (5-20% of total budget)

**Use Cases:**
- Unexpected technical challenges
- Market changes
- Competitive responses
- Regulatory compliance
- Team retention

### Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Start with MVP:** Focus on core features only
2. **Cloud Cost Optimization:** Right-sizing, reserved instances
3. **Team Efficiency:** Remote work, flexible hours
4. **Marketing Efficiency:** Data-driven campaigns, A/B testing
5. **Vendor Partnerships:** Revenue-sharing instead of fixed costs
