## Required Documents

### Mandatory Documents for All Programs

#### 1. Pitch Deck (10-15 slides)

**Structure:**
1. Title slide (name, logo, contacts)
2. Problem (what we solve)
3. Solution (how we solve)
4. Market (size and opportunity)
5. Business model (how we make money)
6. Competitive advantages
7. Team
8. Traction (if any)
9. Financial forecasts
10. Funding request
11. Use of funds

**Requirements:**
- Visually appealing
- Clear and concise
- In English
- PDF format
- Maximum 15 slides

---

#### 2. Business Plan (15-30 pages)

**Structure:**
1. Executive Summary (1-2 pages)
2. Company description
3. Product/service description
4. Market analysis
5. Competitive analysis
6. Marketing strategy
7. Operational plan
8. Management and team
9. Financial plan
10. Risk analysis
11. Appendices

**Requirements:**
- Professional formatting
- Detailed financial forecasts
- In English and German
- PDF format

---

#### 3. Financial Model

**Content:**
- Revenue forecasts for 3-5 years
- Expense forecasts
- Cash flow forecast
- Break-even point
- Scenarios (optimistic, realistic, pessimistic)
- Funding needs
- Use of funds

**Format:**
- Excel file with formulas
- Description of assumptions
- Charts and visualizations

---

#### 4. One-Pager (1 page)

**Content:**
- Project name
- Brief description (2-3 sentences)
- Problem and solution
- Key metrics
- Team (briefly)
- Contacts

**Requirements:**
- One page
- Visually appealing
- Easy to read
- PDF format

---

#### 5. Team Description

**Content:**
- Resume of each team member
- Roles and responsibilities
- Experience and achievements
- Education
- Recommendations (optional)

**Format:**
- PDF document
- Professional photos
- Contact information

## Key Incubator Questions

### Team Questions

1. **Who are you and why you?**
   - Preparation: Emphasize team experience and competencies

2. **What are the roles in the team?**
   - Preparation: Clearly describe role distribution

3. **How do you work together?**
   - Preparation: Describe team work processes

### Product Questions

1. **What problem do you solve?**
   - Preparation: Clearly formulate the problem

2. **How is your solution better than competitors?**
   - Preparation: Prepare comparison with competitors

3. **Do you have an MVP or prototype?**
   - Preparation: Show demo or screenshots

### Market Questions

1. **What is the market size?**
   - Preparation: Provide TAM, SAM, SOM

2. **How do you plan to attract customers?**
   - Preparation: Describe marketing strategy

3. **What is your business model?**
   - Preparation: Clearly describe revenue sources

### Financial Questions

1. **How much money do you need?**
   - Preparation: Justify funding amount

2. **How will you use the investments?**
   - Preparation: Detailed plan for use of funds

3. **When will you reach break-even?**
   - Preparation: Show financial forecasts

## Recommendations

### General Recommendations

1. **Start early:** Preparation takes time, start 2-3 months before deadlines

2. **Adapt materials:** Each incubator has its own requirements, adapt documents

3. **Get feedback:** Show materials to mentors and get feedback

4. **Be honest:** Don't exaggerate metrics and forecasts

5. **Prepare for rejections:** Not all applications will be accepted, this is normal

### Specific Recommendations

1. **For EXIST:** Emphasize university connection and innovativeness

2. **For Techstars:** Emphasize global potential and scalability

3. **For Rocket Internet:** Emphasize business model and growth potential

4. **For regional programs:** Emphasize regional connection and job creation
